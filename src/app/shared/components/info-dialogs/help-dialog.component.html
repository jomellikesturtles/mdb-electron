MDB Help Center

How do I scan my local media?

Go to your library scanner from the settings or navigation menu, select the folders where you keep your media files, and initiate the scan. MDB will recursively identify your files using local filenames and fetch details from TMDB.

Can I watch movies offline?

Yes! Once local metadata is cached and torrents are downloaded or local streaming paths are mapped, you can access your collections without an active internet connection.

Where is my library data stored?

MDB uses a local embedded NeDB database located in the application directory. All of your preferences, bookmarks, and scanned library details remain secure on your machine.

Missing Movie Details?

Make sure your file names contain the movie title followed by the release year in parenthesis (e.g. The Matrix (1999).mp4). This helps our parser match correctly.

Streaming Issues?

If you experience buffering during torrent playback, make sure you have enough active seeders and check your network firewalls to ensure WebTorrent ports aren't blocked.
